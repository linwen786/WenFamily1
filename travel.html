Driving directions


(click map for driving directions)

Thomas Fogarty Winery is located on the east side of Highway 35 (Skyline Blvd.), 4.5 miles south of Highway 84 (Woodside Rd.) and 2.7 miles north of Page Mill Road.

NOTE: Please budget approximately 45-60 minutes of driving time from the San Jose / Sunnyvale areas due to possibly slow traffic on windy mountain roads.

  • From Highway 280 (North or South) take the Highway 84 exit (Woodside Road) driving west toward the mountains. Go through the town of Woodside, the road then becomes steep, narrow and winding.
  • Keep heading up the mountain.
  • When you reach the top at the intersection of Highway 35 (Skyline Blvd.) make a sharp left turn onto 35 (Skyline Blvd.).
  • Drive 4.5 miles and the winery is on the left, across the street from the entrance of Yerba Buena Nursery.

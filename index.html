Ericson Wen & Jenny Le: The Marriage!

Throw in some Christmas lights in the summer of 2004 on the beautiful island of Maui, and what have you got? A masterful plan devised by Ericson to capture the love of his life, Jenny Many don't know the full story of how it happened. The picture below displays the Christmas lights arranged by Ericson and a friend for the romantic proposal. When Jenny was brought to this scene on Mount Haleakala, Ericson got down on one knee and flipped the light switch on. What did the lights say? It said, "You Marry Me". Jenny thought, "That's funny. Sounds more like a demand than a question." After the technical difficulties were resolved so that the lights read like a question instead of caveman-speak, Jenny ecstatically said "yes!". And so the journey begins!


Please join us on Saturday, September 17th, 2005 to mark the union of this happy couple. The ceremony and reception will take place promptly at 1:45 PM at the Thomas Fogarty Winery in Woodside, CA. The ceremony will be held outdoors on the lawn, while the reception will take place in the Pavilion and Hill House. Please visit www.fogartywinery.com for more information about the wedding site location.
